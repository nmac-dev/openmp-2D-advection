### The simulation input parameters

20		| maxQueueLength
1		| numServicePoints
100		| closingTime
6		| stdDiviation
2		| csmrFrequency
8		| csmrWaitTolerance
10		| servicePointSpeed

### The  simulation output parameters

8		| Number of fulfilled customers
0		| Number of unfulfilled customers
9		| Number of timed-out customers
-31		| Average time for fulfilled customers
0		| Time it to serve all customers from closing
